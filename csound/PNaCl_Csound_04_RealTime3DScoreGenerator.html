Csound HTML Tutorial 04

Dunes v 4

By Michael Gogins

2015

This tutorial demonstrates an interactive composition that uses WebGL to generate an animated, three-dimensional fractal that also plays a Csound score in real time.

Load this Web page. You may need to experiment with different levels of zoom to obtain the best appearance. Look at the Csound messages and wait for Csound to finish loading. Click on the "Play" button, then click on the ""Play chord" buttons. Each button applies a chord trasformation and plays the result. The == button repeats the existing chord. You should normally wait for the sequence to finish playing before clicking again.

You can play the piece, stop it from playing, use the sliders to control the sounds of the instruments as they play, and see Csound's diagnostic messages. You may find this piece immediately below. Go ahead and try it -- it will play on version 31 or later of Google's Chrome browser for OS X, Windows, or Linux. Examine the source code to see how the piece works. There are a few comments to explain what is going on.
